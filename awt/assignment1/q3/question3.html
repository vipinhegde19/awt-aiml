MCA Department

Vision

To be a center of excellence in computing, nurturing professionals to innovate and excel in technology.

Mission

To empower students with the latest knowledge in computing, fostering a culture of research and ethical practices.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Think critically, work creatively, communicate effectively, and become technologically competent.
  • Update themselves in areas and technologies relevant to their career.
  • Function in supportive/leadership roles with ethical responsibilities and regard for societal needs.
  • Develop teamwork ability, lead initiatives, and manage tasks effectively.

Program Outcomes (POs)

  1. Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering.
  2. Identify, formulate, research, and analyze complex engineering problems.
  3. Design systems, components, or processes within realistic constraints (economic, environmental, social, etc.).
  4. Design and conduct experiments, analyze and interpret data.
  5. Apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern IT tools.
  6. Assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues.
  7. Understand the global, economic, environmental, and societal impact of engineering solutions.
  8. Uphold professional and ethical responsibility.
  9. Function effectively as an individual, member, or leader in multidisciplinary teams.
  10. Communicate effectively.
  11. Recognize the need for lifelong learning.
  12. Stay informed on contemporary issues.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Empower students to excel in Information Technology with credibility, integrity, and ethical standards.
  • Adopt research practices and trending technologies like AI, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Data Analytics to solve real-world problems.
